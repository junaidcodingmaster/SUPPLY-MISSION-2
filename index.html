Story

You are a sergeant on a military peacekeeping mission. Your task is to drop a package in a designated red drop zone. The package contains mission-critical items and is very important to be delivered at the exact location for the success of the mission.
Everyone before you has failed to finish the task. The success of the mission now depends on you.

Fullscreen

How to play game ?

Press down arrow button on your keyboard to drop down the package.(in desktop).

Tap anywhere to drop down the package.(in mobile).

***IMPORTANT***
If you Press down arrow button on your keyboard to drop down the package.(in desktop). and it is not working Then take your cusier to game and right click then the down arrow button will work.
